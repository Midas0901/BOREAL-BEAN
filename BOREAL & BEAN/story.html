Our Story

Boreal & Bean Our founders, avid backcountry campers, were inspired by the crisp morning air, the scent of damp earth, and the crackle of pine wood in Algonquin Provincial Park. They realized that while coffee beans cannot grow in Canada's freezing winters, the spirit of the coffee experience could still be entirely homegrown.

We partner directly with sustainable, fair-trade family farms in Central America and East Africa. Once the green beans arrive in Canada, we slow-roast them in our eco-roastery in Ontario using a low-emission air roaster.

From wild-harvested ingredients in our signature lattes to reclaimed Canadian wood used in our café interior, Boreal & Bean is a tribute to the Canadian wilderness.

Our Core Values

100% Canadian Craftsmanship — Our café furniture and bars are handcrafted using reclaimed Canadian wood, including British Columbia Douglas Fir and Ontario Barnwood. Every mug is custom-thrown by independent Canadian ceramic artists.

Zero-Trace Green Mission — We use 100% compostable packaging, and for every 50 cups of coffee sold, we help fund the planting of one native tree seedling in areas affected by Canadian wildfires.

Wild-Infused Flavors — Our specialty drinks use natural Canadian ingredients such as maple syrup, wildflower honey, birch syrup, and ethically foraged spruce tips.
