Our Origin

What is Boreal & Bean

"Born from the crisp air, damp earth, and crackling pine fires of Algonquin."

Boreal & Bean was born from a deep love for Canada's northern wilderness. Our founders, passionate backcountry campers, were inspired by the untouched boreal forests of Ontario.

Although coffee cannot grow in Canada, we bring the spirit of the north to every cup. We source beans from sustainable farms in Central America and East Africa, then slow-roast them in our eco-roastery in Ontario.

We honor the land with 100% Canadian craftsmanship — using reclaimed wood, local ceramics, fully compostable packaging, and our "Bring-A-Tree" initiative, planting one tree for every 50 cups sold.

Our drinks feature unique wild-infused flavors using Canadian maple syrup, birch syrup, and foraged spruce tips — delivering more than just coffee, but a true taste of the wilderness.
