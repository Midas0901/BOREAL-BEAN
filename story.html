Our Story

Boreal & Bean was born from a deep love for Canada’s northern wilderness. Our founders, passionate backcountry campers, were inspired by the crisp morning air, the scent of damp earth, and the crackle of pine wood in Algonquin Provincial Park.

They realized that while coffee beans cannot grow in Canada’s freezing winters, the spirit of the coffee experience could still be entirely homegrown. We source high-quality beans from sustainable farms in Central America and East Africa, then slow-roast them in our eco-roastery in Ontario using a low-emission air roaster.

Every element at Boreal & Bean reflects our commitment to the land — from wild-harvested ingredients in our signature lattes to reclaimed Canadian wood used in our café interior. Boreal & Bean is more than just a café — it is a tribute to the Canadian wilderness and a celebration of slow, mindful living.

OUR PROMISE

CORE VALUES

100% CANADIAN CRAFTSMANSHIP

Our café furniture and bars are handcrafted using reclaimed Canadian wood, including British Columbia Douglas Fir and Ontario Barnwood. Every mug is custom-thrown by independent Canadian ceramic artists.

ZERO-TRACE GREEN MISSION

We use 100% compostable packaging, and for every 50 cups of coffee sold, we help fund the planting of one native tree seedling in areas affected by Canadian wildfires.

WILD-INFUSED FLAVORS

Our specialty drinks use natural Canadian ingredients such as maple syrup, wildflower honey, birch syrup, and ethically foraged spruce tips.
